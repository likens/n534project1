How To Play
Clicking this button or pressing the Escape key will pause your game.
Your score will increase the farther and longer you go on each track. Collect Powerups to go faster!
Your lives will decrease every time you hit a Blocker. It's Game Over when you run out!
Avoid all Blockers! These will cause you to lose lives.
Collect all Powerups! These will increase your speed and score.
Type the indicated key on the left or right of your Racer to avoid Blockers and collect Powerups!
